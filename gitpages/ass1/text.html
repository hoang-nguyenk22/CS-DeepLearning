Text Classification: Odoc Tagger

🏆 Project Summaries & Achievements

Author: Vũ Hoàng Tùng

Full Report: Report on Text Classification.pdf

Abstract

Classifying long-form documents within a high-dimensional label space (10^3–10^6) has been always a formiddable challenge, yet has been needed for solution more than ever, due to the non-stop accumulation of documents, papers of complex domain with many topic overlap. As such a field called XMC. This study benchmarks BiLSTM-Attention and Transformer-based Encoders on their ability to handle semantic overlap and document length in the Eurlex57k dataset.

Key Achievements

  • Pipeline Design: Developed a multi-segment Transformer backbone (1024 tokens), expanding context window for long legal documents.
  • Deep Learning Application: Combining finetuning with ultilized classification head training, increasing Macro-F1 from 0.03 (Vanilla) to 0.25. page 27
  • Semantic Precision: Demonstrated superior entity detection (e.g., “Singapore”, “Commission”, "Commitee") using Attention Rollout to ignore redundant legal jargon.page 28

Methodology and Technical Highlights

  • Context Window: Expanded from 512 to 1024 tokens via multi-segment feature fusion (Title + Body) + (Recitals). [page 20]
  • Loss Engineering: Implemented Asymmetric Loss (ASL) to suppress negative-class dominance (4,187 negative labels per doc). [page 20. 21]
  • Long-Tail Recovery: Apply Weight balancing for rare labels (Consist of < 0.001 %) [page 20, 21]
  • Training Strategy: Utilized Semantic Warm-start finetune strategy to guide the model’s “semantic compass” from Epoch 1. [page 21]
  • Efficiency: Achieved near SOTA-level precision ($P@1 > 0.88$) using a lightweight 33M parameter backbone, significantly reducing inference latency. [page 27]

Limitations

  • Defeated by RoBERTa (upon trained it uploaded as LegalBERT) on nearly every other Metric than Macro F1 and P@3 due to lower Recall rate (we likely need to moderate weights better).
  • Confidence logs shows that models only show certainty in top 3-5 labels.
  • Finetune boost final precision strongly however are fast to concave. Maybe Mini-LM-L12-v2 has been salturated upon too many epoch. We fix this by dynamic training stratergy, however requires many epochs for head to adapt

*Note: Our results prove that a "Lightweight Transformer + Advanced Loss" approach still yields high-precision results while maintaining resource efficiency.

On-the-fry Analysis

For Transformer, we provide visualization using Attention Rollout. Basically, the gradient of the final layer will be taken and plotted with mapped tokens to show significant tokens within provided texts, allowing us to understand the model's "internal compass."

This analysis proves that model are capable to focus more on entities like Singapore and commission (shown as red), while ignoring dates, numbers or grammar word (shown as white), however both attention rollout and weight shows that model is not confident when there are more than 3 tags.
